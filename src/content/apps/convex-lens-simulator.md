An interactive ray diagram tool for exploring how a convex (converging) lens forms images.

## How to use it

- **Drag the object** (the arrow) left and right to change its distance from the lens
- Watch the **image** update in real time — position, size, and orientation all change
- The **three principal rays** are drawn to show exactly how the image forms

## What you can observe

| Object position | What happens to the image |
|---|---|
| Far from lens (beyond 2f) | Small, inverted, real image on the other side |
| At 2f | Same size, inverted, real image at 2f |
| Between f and 2f | Enlarged, inverted, real image far away |
| Inside f (magnifying glass) | Upright, enlarged virtual image on the same side |
