
Most people have heard of the letter pi. If you did maths classes at school you may remember... 3.142, right?
You likely memorized this number so that you could solve such useful problems as...

Calculate the perimeter of a circular field of radius 10m

  circumference = 2 x pi * 10 = 20 x 3.142 = 62.84m

I bet that served you well in your life post school, right?

But, what is pi ?  Just some number that mathematicims made up right?

Actually, the truth is much more mystical. No one, "invented" or made up this number. 
It's a property of the universe itself and, amazingly, it's one that you could, in principle,
calculate, or at least estimate, at your kitchen table with nothing more sophisticated than
pen and paper, a compass and a ruler.

What's more, it doesn't have to be equal to 3.142... that's its valiue in our universe, but
it could be something different. If one day, you ever happen to wander through a multi-dimensional
teleporter and arent sure where you have ended up. Sit yourself down at a kitchen table and 
estimate the value of pi. If it's not 3.142... then you'll know that you've strayed into another
universe.
