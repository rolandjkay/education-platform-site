Known issue fixed during this session
--------------------------------------
Having both gcse-physics-lenses.md and gcse-physics-lenses.mdx (or
pinhole-camera.md and pinhole-camera.mdx) in the same content directory caused
Astro 5's glob() loader to silently drop all entries from the collection.
Fix: deleted the stub .md files. The .mdx files are now the sole source of truth.
